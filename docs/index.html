🎴 Link your EDH Play account

Only needed if you want pods to launch rooms under your own EDH Play account. Otherwise the bot already creates rooms for you — just click the join links.

Step 1 — install the button (once). Drag this to your bookmarks bar:

⬇ EDH Play → bot

No bookmarks bar? Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or ⌘+Shift+B (Mac) to show it, then drag.

Step 2 — use it.

  1. Go to edhplay.com and make sure you're logged in.
  2. Click the EDH Play → bot bookmark — it copies your tokens.
  3. In Discord, run /link → Enter tokens → paste into the first box. Done.

Your tokens are copied to your clipboard and only go into the bot's /link box — they never pass through this page or anyone else.
